Plot Summary

"Idiocracy" (2006) follows Joe Bauers, an average American who is accidentally frozen and wakes up 500 years in the future to find society has become dangerously unintelligent. Joe becomes the smartest person alive and is forced to help fix a collapsing world suffering from poor education, failing infrastructure, and widespread misinformation. The film blends satire and dystopian comedy to highlight how neglecting knowledge and critical thinking can harm future generations.
